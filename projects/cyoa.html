Choose Your Own Adventure Project

This is probably one of the more less impressive projects that I have made in SEP, but I have to put it here anyways (lol).

The purpose of this project was the practice linking files. Me and my partner Junhua had to link different HTML files in different subdirectories. In the end we had a total of over 10 files including different situations for our Choose Your Own Adventure. Overall the hardest part of this project was the communication, but me and my partner addressed that challenge pretty well so we had very little problems while making the webpage. This project was pretty easy so I hope to do more difficult ones in the future.
